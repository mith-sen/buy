## The Problem

Consumers buy packaged products daily without understanding what's in them, which ingredients are harmful, or how their choices affect their health and wallet. Product labels are full of jargon â€” and shopping bills offer zero health context.

---

##  What It Does

**Product Label Analysis**
- Detects unsafe or harmful ingredients
- Assigns a health risk rating (Safe / Moderate / Not Recommended)
- Evaluates environmental impact of packaging
- Suggests healthier store-bought and homemade alternatives

**Shopping Bill Analysis**
- Breaks down items with a health impact rating per product
- Identifies potential cost savings
- Recommends better alternatives for flagged items
- Generates an overall basket health score
