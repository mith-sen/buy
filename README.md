## Overview
Consumers often purchase packaged products without clearly understanding their nutritional value, potential health risks, or environmental impact, as product labels and purchase bills contain complex information that is difficult to interpret quickly. This results in unhealthy consumption choices, unnoticed harmful ingredients, and lack of awareness about better alternatives.

## What the System Does
- Identifies purchased items from a product label or shopping bill
- Evaluates nutritional quality and potential health risks
- Assesses environmental impact of packaging
- Provides simple explanations and recommendations
- Suggests healthier and homemade alternatives
